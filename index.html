Our Story

A Taste of America in Amsterdam

Nestled in the vibrant Warmoesstraat, Grizzly brings the soul of American dining to the heart of Amsterdam. Our kitchen celebrates the rich traditions of American comfort food — from flame-grilled burgers and slow-smoked spare ribs to hearty breakfasts and craft beers.

Every dish is prepared with passion, using quality ingredients and time-honored recipes. Whether you are craving a classic Full English Breakfast, a juicy Black Angus Burger, or our legendary fall-off-the-bone ribs, Grizzly delivers an unforgettable dining experience.
